Clubs


Wordsworth Club:

The power over a global language is very significant to survive in the rapid advancing world . Communication becomes futile when language becomes a barrier. Our enthusiastic learners get together to work on to develop their efficiency in the English language by means of conduction of various activities like Just a minute talk, extempore, debates, spellathons and many more, to enhance the language and vocabulary skills.

The learners are given various opportunities to take part in inter and intra school competitions which raises their confidence and motivates them to learn and define themselves as an individual.

Universal Club:

Geography, history and civics are few subjects which many of us didn't fancy studying but think how imprudent would it seem to visit a new place without having no knowledge about it or to question the rules and policies of a country without being aware of its formation. In this club, our learners come together and participate in activities related to the historical events or demographics of a place or the constitutional events of a country by way of virtual tours over the gadgets or by forming an unreal parliament and describe its functions. This makes the students fascinated towards the subject knowledge which appeared dull to them and also such exposure helps in developing wise and responsible global citizens.

Einstein Club:

Science is one the most intriguing subject to most of the learners. Its the power to create and to destroy ,must be used wisely to make wonders. Our learners are open to learning and practicing principles of science by conducting experiments or by carrying out activities like small medical camps at school premise or science exhibition. The learners are zealous while performing various activities. We expose our learners to multiple activities as we see them to be scholars in the science stream and vibrant career options in the future

Ramanujan Club:

Take a break from textbook math and enjoy yourself! Interesting activities are conducted which offers a host of educational benefits, plus you build relationships and make memories. Activities like puzzle solving, detective games, number maze and many more help the learners in exactly the right frame of mind for learning difficult things. Mathematics is mental play, the essence of creative problem solving. Math is a game, playing with ideas.

Premchand Club :

Hindi-Club is to enhance the creativity of our scholars. The Club allows scholars to create their own speeches, nukkad-natak, and plays, compose poetry and also write essays to express their thoughts and feelings. Nukkad-Natak is also an integral part of the Hindi Club where scholars are not only being made aware of important social issues but they are encouraged to take on the responsibility of spreading its awareness. The Club will also conducts activities like debates and extempore to develop thinking and analysing skills in our scholars.

Swaranjali Club :

The music club is a place for students to explore, create and share their individual musical interests. Students share playlists, have discussions on musical issues, create projects individually and in groups and perform for each other. It is open to all students with any level and type of background in music. This club strives to promote all student participation in the contribution and enjoyment of their musical abilities/talents and to provide an outlet for all students interested in music.

Technomaniac Club :

Learners today are digital natives, they embrace technology and so a different approach can be used to teach kids computer science than what is used in the classroom. Computer club allows students to take more ownership over their own learning, which will grow them as lifelong learners.It truly enhance the learning experience for students in the computer club as they learn the basics of computer science concepts, which will not only provide a focus on computer science concepts for computer club activities, but it will also further develop your thinking and problem solving skills.

Creative Club :

Having an Art Club is a great way for students to be involved in art . When our time with them is limited in the classroom, an art club can serve as a nice supplement for students who can’t get enough art. An art club also allows more opportunities and freedom to try new things with students. Crafting with learners is all about having fun, experimenting, taking ideas that you see and adapting them to suit what you have available and what you think your children would enjoy most. It is always a good thing to be creative.

Campus Life

The Little Kingdom Senior Secondary School is committed to providing a healthy space for young pupil to thrive both in the classroom and beyond. We value student and faculty support at the annual events, organized large in scope. From the smallest gesture to the biggest requirement, at L.K.S. every student has the opportunity to make an impact on the community in his or her own ways.

Students have leadership opportunities as they are encouraged to get involved in some of the many organization offered like the activity clubs formed for literary, arts, dance and music, athletics and technology which lay emphasis in holistic development of the child.

We look to create our campus not as a backdrop but as a foreground for the child’s future.
