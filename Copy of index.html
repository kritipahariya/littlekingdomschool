    • Admissions

      LKS follows standardized admission procedures as recommended in the CBSE guidelines (M.P. & Central Govt.)
      Admission for Nursery to XII can be obtained only if there is a vacancy in the particular class. All applications for admission must be submitted latest by the 1st of April (new session).
      Previous School Records, Merit Certificates, Transfer Certificate, Birth Certificate and Certificates of Special Achievements must be presented at the time of registration.
